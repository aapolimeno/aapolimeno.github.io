# Lab 2: Publishing RDF data  


## The following **classes** and **properties** are defined by the **apvocab** vocabulary: 


### Classes 
- `apvocab:Person` - A human 
- `apvocab:Organization` - An organization

### Properties 
- `fullName` - First and last name of a person
- `worksAt` - Organization a person is employed at
- `jobTitle` - The title of the job a person has
- `favoriteAnimal` - The favorite animal of a person
- `favoriteFood` - The favorite food of a person
- `knows` - Lists others that are known by a person
